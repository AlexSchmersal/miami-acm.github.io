About Us

Miami University ACM Student Chapter (a.k.a. Miami ACM) is the local student chapter of Association for Computing Machinery for Miami University. Miami ACM, closely corrdinating with Miami University Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering, is interested in the academic and professional development for students pursuing their career in computer science and software engineering, pursuant to ACM's slogan Advancing Computing as a Science & Profession.
